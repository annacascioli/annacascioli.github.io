Anna Cascioli

I am a PhD student in the Mathematics Cluster of Excellence at the University of Münster. I am a member of the research group Model Theory and Groups, working under the supervision of Katrin Tent. I am also cosupervised by Shirly Geffen in the Operator Algebras group.

I am interested in geometric group theory and its intersections with operator algebras. In particular, I am trying to build new C*-simple groups from Burnside-like quotients of groups. In order to do so, I am currently studying the structure of free Burnside groups through small cancellation theory.
