Anna Cascioli

I am a PhD student in the Mathematics Cluster of Excellence at the University of Münster. I am a member of the research group Model Theory and Groups, working under the supervision of Prof. Katrin Tent. I am also cosupervised by Prof. Shirly Geffen in the Operator Algebras group.

My main interests lie in geometric group theory and its intersections with operator algebras. In particular, I am trying to build new C*-simple groups from Burnside-like quotients of groups. In order to do so, I am currently studying the structure of free Burnside groups through small cancellation theory.

Before coming to Münster, I completed my Master's Degree in the ALGANT Program: I spent my first year at the University of Padova with Prof. Andrea Lucchini and my second year at the University of Regensburg, where I wrote my thesis under the supervision of Prof. Clara Löh on Profinite Rigidity and Amenability. You may find my thesis here.
